Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about booking and training sessions.

What should I bring to my session?

Bring comfortable athletic clothing, soccer cleats or athletic shoes, water, and any specific equipment you'd like to work with. All training equipment is provided.

How far in advance should I book?

It's recommended to book at least 24-48 hours in advance to ensure availability. Weekend slots tend to fill up quickly.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24-hour notice for cancellations. Late cancellations may be subject to a fee. We understand emergencies happen and will work with you.

Can I reschedule my session?

Yes, you can reschedule your session up to 24 hours before the scheduled time through the booking calendar or by contacting us directly.
